How this works?

Follow this simple user journey and you are all set to recycle your old device in a responsible way and at the same time, earn exciting rewards and maybe a chance to upgrade to a surface device.

User who wants to upgrade to a new device goes to Win Settings, fills up device working condition details

User is shown their expected rewards and appropriate certified vendor details 

User ships their device* to the certified refurbishing vendor OR E-waste disposal vendor

User gets discounts/rewards on the purchase of a Surface

User avails the discount to take home a Surface
